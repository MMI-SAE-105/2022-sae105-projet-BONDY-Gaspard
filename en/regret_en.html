Again, another phenomenon similar to the article on the hope to save the planet (we invite you to go and see it) is global warming. One of the consequences of human actions on the globe, but what is global warming?

What is global warming?

It is a global phenomenon of climate change characterised by a general increase in temperature. This phenomenon is mainly caused by human action. It also modifies at the same timeweather balanceas well asthe ecosystem. Parmi les actions humaines ont comptent industrialization, the release of greenhouse gases but also pollution. There is still a very large list that influences global warming, but we are not going to write it here.

The causes of global warming are mainly greenhouse gases which come mainly from the production of heat and electricity but also fuel for transport, especially for cars.

It's nice to talk about things that hurt, but how can we fight global warming?

How do we deal with this enormous struggle that unfortunately lies ahead of us?

How to fight global warming?

It would therefore be necessary to reduce the production of greenhouse gases, and to do this, we should start by using only renewable and non-polluting energy such as : energy from wind turbines, solar panels or water power. Consideration could also be given to the use of public transport in cities or even better the bike or his feet. Use other means of transport, all except the car. In order to reduce global warming, every human being should react and become aware that their actions will spreadover generations of generations.
