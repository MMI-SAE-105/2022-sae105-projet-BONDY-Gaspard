As you can see today, the world is going badly, the planet is going badly. Something must be done! In our opinion, saving the planet would be a hope, the hope of giving future generations a clean and healthy planet. But let's face it, it's not looking good.

Let's start by asking a simple question: What is Save the Planet?

What is Save the Planet?

This action is about doing something, it's about doing something no matter who we are, no matter how old we are, how tall we are, how heavy we are. We can all do something, from the smallest infant to the oldest person, we can all intervene to save the planet of tomorrow.

Saving the planet is first and foremost a great fight, given by great men and women. It is the fact of wanting to save it, above all to repair the bad actions that Man has done in the past. We can quote for example : pollution, deforestation, air & soil pollution, species extinction and many others.All these things have only one point of origin, and that is man. Without him, the planet would be fine. But today the countdown to this great battle has begun!

But how do you save the planet?

How to save the planet?

To save the planet, we should first stop polluting, but that's easier said than done. Pollution is one of the main causes of problems for the planet. One example is the large seas of plastic that are formed by the many plastics that are thrown into the environment every day. Tons and tons all over the world. To save it, it will be necessary that each human being faces good gestures: sorting waste, not wasting water, electricity and much more.

Some countries, such as France, are beginning to realise the current state of the planet and are putting measures in place. But others have not yet reacted, such as the United States.

We must act quickly, time is short!
