Below, you can find the website's glossary: if you don't understand a word, or if you want to know more about it, it's here!

Spoil/Spoiler Alert:

Spoil someone's fun by telling them about a film/series they haven't seen yet.

FPS:

A term used in video games to define the player's view, in this case FPS stands for First Person Shooter.

TPS:

A term used in video games to define the player's view, in this case TPS stands for Third Person Shooter.

Free-To-Play :

The term "Free-To-Play" means "free to play". It is associated with a video game when it is freely available. It is also associated with the abbreviation "F2P".
